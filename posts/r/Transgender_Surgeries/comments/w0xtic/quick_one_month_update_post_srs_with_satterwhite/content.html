I always feel it's good to share results as we go so the community can see how things progress :). It's not always easy to find results, so figured I'd share mine.

The good:

  • Wound breakdown is healing quite rapidly; just a little bit left on the bottom left labia majora. Compared to my last post two weeks ago, things look way better. I anticipate it should be filled in within the next 3-4 weeks.
  • Sensation is fantastic - was able to orgasm twice in the last 5 days or so. Surgeon said ok to masturbate, just keep things out of the vaginal canal. Orgasms themselves haven't been as powerful as before, but I assume there is still numbness to work through.
  • Dilation is getting easier. I'm still holding at the same depth (~11.5-12cm) on the blue dilator. I'm holding off on sizing up to green until wound breakdown is completely healed to prevent continued aggravation of the area.
  • Incision lines overall feel like they are healing quite well.

​

What I'm not yet happy with:

  • I don't really have a well defined clitoral hood or labia minora. I know at this stage in the healing, the swelling masks a lot of it. Surgeon says he can very slightly see parts of the minora near the clitoris itself, but I can't. Maybe something to handle in the future on revision? Open to thoughts from the community!
  • The edges of the labia majora are quite prominent and easy to see (well defined border that you can obviously feel at the incisions); my hope is that over time, this will "blend out" into my body so there isn't this stark line.
  • Labia majora skin feels pretty loose. Guessing this is left over from the swelling and stretching, and once again, hoping it will resolve itself over time as the skin tightens back up.
  • The clitoris itself looks a bit funky as there is a defined line in the middle. Surgeon says its due to the current suturing, and once that is absorbed/dissolved, this will resolve itself.

I'm working on getting in to see a pelvic floor physical therapist, as I have heard it does wonders for dilation. Also going to start using silicone ointment on the incisions to prevent scarring

I'm trying to keep some perspective for myself (though it's tough); I never had crippling bottom dysphoria (lucky me!), so I'm not looking for a picture perfect vagina, but one that can pass well enough to have sexual relations.

Also fun fact - Dr. Satterwhite took leftover tissue from my urethra and put it into the vagina to give it that "vagina pink" color, which I thought was kind of a cool detail.
