Hi all, I thought I'd just make a post to share some things I've learned while booking my SRS at the Suporn Clinic (with Dr. Bank if anyone's curious).

Booking while under 18

You won't have any issues booking if you're under 18 (I booked when I was 17), as long as you will be 18 at the time of surgery. I know people have had questions about this, so hopefully this will clear it up!

Financial logistics while under 18

You likely won't be able to do a wire transfer while you're under 18 (I could not, and I assume other banks would be the same), and you likely won't be able to use any other global transfer services either. In order to book a date at the Suporn clinic, you will need to pay a 20% deposit within 21 days, so if you will be under 18 during this time, you'll need to transfer the money to an adult in your life and get them to make the transfer for you.

Parental consent while under 20

You will not only need parental consent for surgery in Thailand if you're under 20, but at least for the Suporn Clinic, you will need a parent to accompany you to sign the papers in person. As far as I know this information isn't online, I found out through contacting the clinic. Obviously this will be an extra financial burden, as well as a big ask for your parent(s), so make sure to plan accordingly.

I haven't asked the clinic about parental consent for revisions, but I'd imagine it would be the same. You'll want to either plan to be able to bring a parent for a revision, or book your surgery at a date where you'll be 20 during the free revision period. I chose to do the second.

Edit: I just got confirmation from the clinic that a parent would need to accompany you for a revision if you're under 20.

​

Hopefully this information is helpful! I'll try to update this post if I find anything more that's relevant.
