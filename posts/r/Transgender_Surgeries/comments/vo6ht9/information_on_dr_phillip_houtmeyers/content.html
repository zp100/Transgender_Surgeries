I met him and had been quite disappointed.

  • He didn't seem very professional to me.
  • He showed me a very limited number of results and argued that the rest is on his computer at home...
  • The results were not very convincing.
  • Regarding his method, he actually does PI most of the time and PI-PPT very exceptionnaly (I am not even sure it is "pull-through" actually) and actually discourages from it because he believes it doesn't bring an added value to patients, (which, to be honest, is something some other surgeons say as well, and why they mostly do variations of PI rather than PPT).

As you stated yourself, there are very few feedbacks on him. I had managed to find only one of his former patients.
