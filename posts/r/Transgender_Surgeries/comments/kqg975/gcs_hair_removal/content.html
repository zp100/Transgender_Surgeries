You might want to mark your post NSFW 'cause there may be pics of pubic area in responses. I for one am not posting any pics until the post is marked NFSW 'cause I'd hate for someone to be reading this in an unsafe place and running into unexpected contents.

Thoughts-wise, you said "four treatments". Were they laser or electrolysiis? And over how long a period? Hair grows in cycles and at any moment can be "dormant" i.e. the hair follicle is there but there is no hair growing and visible so not removable via laser or electrolysis. A quick search seems to indicate that pubic hair's cycle ranges up to a few weeks, while other types of hair can be dormant for several months.

You didn't say how far off is your GCS. Me, I'm taking my time and do as much hair removal for as long as I feel necessary before my GCS. There is no "re-do" for GCS. I want to keep the variables as few and as small as possible. My timeline is also probably a little different than some in that I had electrolysis of my pubic areas for several years a long time ago. My GCS is coming up in August. I am getting more electrolysis to address very minor regrowth. By August, I think I'll be as ready hair-wise as I can ever be.
