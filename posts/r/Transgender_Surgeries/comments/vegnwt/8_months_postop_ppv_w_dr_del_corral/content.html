I leak continuously so I have to wear a pad continuously, or at least a liner. It's a constant drip of slightly viscous orange fluid, I think it's mostly from granulation tissue weeping. There's also some mucus like stuff slowly working out, maybe that's from the peritoneum. I've had to dilate every day since I got out, my routine is once a day, 15 minutes on the smaller blue one and 30 minutes on the larger green one. I've used the huge orange one a few times but don't do it often. Dilation sucks, it hurts and it takes time to get it to depth. It's been getting better though. Skipping dilation for a day or two is a mistake. The few times I've done that I've really paid for it. Super painful and lots of bleeding, takes days to get back to normal. I'm happy with it though, I had severe genital dysphoria and now thats 100% gone, I've never been more comfortable with my body, I actually feel normal for the first time in my life.
