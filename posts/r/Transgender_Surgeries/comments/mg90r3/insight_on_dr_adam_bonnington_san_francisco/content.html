I consulted with him a few weeks ago. He told me he's been doing surgeries for two years, participated in around 80 vaginoplasties and and conducted 20 start to finish; of them, he's seen 30 PPTs, actively participated in 15, and conducted 5 start to finish.

From my layman's point of view he was friendly and well informed, but I personally felt I would be more comfortable going to a surgeon with more experience. His advantage, of course, is that his waitlist is extremely short at 2-3 months, as opposed to 10 for inversion or 12-14 for PPT with his mentor Wittenberg.
