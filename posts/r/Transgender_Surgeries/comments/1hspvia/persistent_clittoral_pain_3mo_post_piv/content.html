I am about 6m out and also was with Gast. At 3m there is still a fair amount of swelling that can make things feel “tight” or like you are still tucking. This can very much be exacerbated with any arousal. It often felt like a dull throbbing pain at its worst. At 3m, I was just starting to be orgasmic but it was not without consequences initially as after sessions it was a little tender. That has stopped and I things became increasing better with time.

Have you had the post op outpatient team look at it yet? As someone else mentioned, it could be a stubborn stitch. I had one that was problematic at around that time that created some very crazy sensations. Luckily it fell out on its own.

You may also have some hypergranulation in that area. Super common. I had some around the urethra and entrance that while stubborn, is now resolved after a number of silver nitrate treatments.

I know you have heard what I am about to say, but I cant emphasize enough how much you are still very much healing. If it really bothers you a quick My Chart inquiry may help to alleviate your concerns or perhaps offer a solution or recommendation to f/u in their clinic.
