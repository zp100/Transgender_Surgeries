All trans Surgeries are subsidised in NZ. You have to have been on hormones for 2 years and over 18. I think they might've changed it but seeing as you're in Auckland just email Auckland Sexual Health Services and they can point you in the right direction.

There are a few surgeons who do top surgery for ftm and only 1 surgeon in NZ who does bottom surgeries for both ftm and mtf, her name is Rita Yang. You can go private and the costs are around 60k nzd as waiting lists for subsidizsd are around 50+ years currently so you're probably better off going overseas for your bottom surgery which will cost half that.

Hope this helps, if you need any other specific info feel free to DM me or ask here - I also live in NZ
