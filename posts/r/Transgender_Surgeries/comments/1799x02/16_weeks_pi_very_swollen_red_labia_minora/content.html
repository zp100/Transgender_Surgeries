I’m not surgeon, but I’ve had my own surgery and I am a registered nurse. It looks like they used your corpus cavernosum solely as your labia minora. The corpus cavernosum is the spongy material our genitals use to draw in blood when aroused. It’s good to have some left after surgery, but it shouldn’t be the sole thing to construct a labia minora.

I’d let your vagina finish healing and get some second opinions if it’s bothering you sis. Your vagina doesn’t look terrible. But I get why you’d be concerned or want other opinions/revisions.
