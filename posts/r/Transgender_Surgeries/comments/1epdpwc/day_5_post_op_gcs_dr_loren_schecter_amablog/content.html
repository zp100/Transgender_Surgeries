Not too but I can speak to this. The pain upon waking up wasn't bad but I was out of it for the first few days. The surgery itself isn't painful its the recovery that is. I had an epidural with diladin which gave me a lot of nausea and vomiting which they gave me nausea medication for. Id say for the vast majority of the week in the hospital pain was on average a 5-7 out of 10. Sitting up/standing up around day 3-4 was definitely a 10 but got easier over time. By day 6 I was walking around majority of the day. (Mainly because I couldn't get comfortable on the bed or chair so next best thing was walk around.

While the first week was hell, just keep in mind that it is a major surgery and takes 3 months to get back to normalcy and a full year to be completely recovered. The first week sucked so much I regretted having it, now 2 months post op I have seldom pain and couldn't be happier.
