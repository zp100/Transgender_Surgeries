I don't know a tremendous amount about Chett's technique. But I do know Suporn (and Bank) go out of their way to preserve as much of the source tissue as possible, so none of it goes to waste and there'll be tissue available for a future revision. Then most revisions they do are to reduce this excess tissue, along with the usual cosmetic "tidying up" that lots of post-op girls would like to get anyway. Plus their first revision is free.

In other words, it's wise to consider surgery at Suporn Clinic to be two-stage from the beginning. First the primary surgery, and then a second cosmetic revision about a year later, all included in the initial price.

I think its a combination of several things.

  • If you're the perfectionist type who going to want a revision you're more likely to pick Suporn due to his reputation for revisions, which then reinforces that reputation.
  • Suporn's quite a bit more expensive so people who go to him tend to have more money and are more likely to be able to afford to go back.
  • I've heard a number of times that Chettawut has refused to do revisions, even for money. Suporn does do even tiny revisions, and until he announced his retirement there was no limit to how many times or when. There's a limit now, one free in 12-18 months I think, but you can still pay after that.
  • The difference in techniques may influence it but I don't know enough about it to be sure.
