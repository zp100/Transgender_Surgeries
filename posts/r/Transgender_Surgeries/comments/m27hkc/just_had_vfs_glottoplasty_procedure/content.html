I just felt like I had a sore throat for a few days. I'm not sure I could ever feel the stitches.

The idea is to do the best you can. You might reflex cough once or twice in your period of silence but as long as you manage it carefully you should be okay.

I don't know about yawning.

If you feel a lot of phlegm try and breathe it out with deep breaths or put honey in your drink to try and break it up.

It's impossible to know if everything has worked until you can speak again.
