I am going with Dr Ferrando at CC, however I know someone who assists with Dr Gupta's surgeries:

-UH has a full medical team, including a (very good) cosmetic surgeon to assist Dr Gupta. Dr Gupta has more of a urology background from what I understand.

-They perform vaginoplasty, phalloplasty, zero depth SRS, and also revisions from other hospitals or revisions on their own work.

-They team is only about a year old, they don't have too many surgeries under their belt.

-Wait list is short, my electrologist has a client whom got in very quick, I believe somewhere between 3 to 6 months lead time. They perform a follicle scrape as well, not sure if they cauterize, but important because the client didn't have a lot of hair removal completed prior to surgery with the given lead time.

Hope some of this helps, I feel more secure with Dr Ferrando right now, because she has performed the surgery so many times (vaginoplasty). This is not to dismiss Dr Gupta's program, I'm sure they have excellent results from what I've heard, it's just limited volume.
