Its called wound dehiscence. This is from my surgery document;

"Wound dehiscence is an opening at the surgical incision. It can occur in the first few weeks after surgery. If you find an opening in the incision, be aware that there is no surgical procedure indicated to close the wound. The healing period will be longer, but the wounds will naturally close from the inside out."
