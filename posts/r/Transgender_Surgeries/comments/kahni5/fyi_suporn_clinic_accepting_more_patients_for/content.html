Archived Link to Annoucement (their news page is changed often)

Looks like the Suporn Clinic will start booking more people for the January-March 2021 time frame. It's more costly (in time and money) than normal as you will need to stay a minimum of 42 days in Thailand, including 14 days of quarantine ahead of your surgery, but it may still be good news for many. The 50,000 THB needed to cover 14 days of quarantine alone come out to an extra USD$1,663.45 (plus cost of food and other expenses) you'd need to budget for as of today's exchange rates.

Looks like you will have to be a resident of a "Green Zone" country as per this list: https://healthserv.net/en3776 . It may be relevant to many people on this sub to note that the United States and United Kingdom are not on this list (albeit for good reason), while Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Korea, Singapore, etc., etc. are.

Apparently you can only book with Dr. Suporn or Dr. Bank currently -- no word on Dr. Prae (P.S. Has anyone on this sub had surgery with her yet?).

They have not re-opened the calendar webpage as of 10 December 2020.

Text of news page:

WE ARE ACCEPTING BOOKINGS FOR JANUARY TO MARCH 2021! – Update 10 December 2020

Dear Clients,

We are delighted to announce that we will be accepting bookings starting from January 2021. Since November, we have undertaken a limited number of operations following the new and tighten controls both from the Thai government and from our partnered hospital. And now, we are more than ready to welcome all interested patients.

If you already have an operation reserved or provisionally reserved AND are resided in the ‘Green Zone’ countries, you have a priority in scheduling your operation with us. Please feel free to contact us to schedule your appointment at your convenience.

Before making an appointment, we would like to inform you of a few important requirements that you must comply. We ask that you please ensure you understand them thoroughly:

  • Travel from ‘Green Zone’ countries only - The Ministry of Public Health (MOPH) requires that international visitors enter Thailand for medical reasons traveled from ‘Green Zone’ countries only. This ‘Green Zone’ is based on your country of residence. Please note that the list of ‘Green Zone’ countries is updated every 2 weeks. You may check your eligibility from the link here: https://healthserv.net/en3776

  • Spend 14 days mandatory self-quarantine at Samitivej Chonburi Hospital (SCH) - Interested patients must stay at SCH, a government qualified state quarantine hospital, prior to the surgery. The rate for 14 days quarantine is 50,000 THB per person per room including full-board. This also includes 3 Covid-19 tests. The cost of quarantine must be paid-in full and directly to SCH prior to your arrival. Guests are welcome, however, you are unable to share a room whilst in quarantine. You must book additional rooms if you are coming with family members or friends.

  • Stay at least 42 days in total - These include 14 full days self-quarantine at SCH and another 28 days or more for operation and recovery.

  • Schedule operation between January - March 2021 - For the first phase of our reopening, we will accept bookings from January - March 2021. You can schedule operations with either Dr. Suporn or Dr. Bank. We will publish the calendar with available dates on our website before end the of 2020. If you decide to book now, please feel free to let us know your preferred date and we will do our best to accommodate that. For operations after April 2021, please wait for the next update.

In addition to the requirements mentioned above, there are extra steps that require your time and expenses to obtain additional travel documents to enter Thailand as followed:

  • Certificate of Entry (COE)
  • Fit-to-Fly or Fit-to-Travel medical certificate
  • COVID-free medical certificate issued 72 hours prior to flight
  • Health insurance covering Covid-19 with coverage over 100,000 USD

We will provide the supporting documents for your COE application. Please study the application guideline from the link here: https://coethailand.mfa.go.th/regis/step?language=en The types of permitted person in your COE application would be ‘Foreigners with Medical Appointment’. Please note that you may need up to 30 days to prepare and process these documents before actual traveling. Should you decide to come, please plan your preparation and processing time accordingly.

In addition to Thailand specific requirements, we ask that you please make sure to study your country specific requirements as it will directly impact your plan beyond what we can assist, particularly:

  • Your country regulations on outward international travel – Some countries impose a ban on international travelling for their citizens, others allow, with special conditions and may require extra documents such as proof of why it is essential that you receive medical treatment aboard. Please make sure you study your countries requirements thoroughly.

  • Your state/national quarantine and required documents on your return journey – You may need to self-quarantine on your way back home or you may need another Covid-19 test before your departing flight, please make sure to consider your time and expenses involved on your return journey.

We are happy and ready to assist you to all the steps necessary to welcome you here in Thailand. For interested patients, please feel free to contact our team at admin@supornclinic.com . We will provide you with more information on the process and documents required to obtain COE. For those who are not ready to proceed at the moment, we will keep you posted on our update and are looking forward to welcoming you soon.

Cosmetic Improvement

Unfortunately we are not yet able to accept booking for cosmetic improvement (CI) because the stringent safety regulations do not allow us to perform any surgery in the Clinic itself. However, as previously notified, we have extended your warranty to compensate for the time we have been closed. We anticipate that we may be able to resume in the second half of 2021.

STAY SAFE
