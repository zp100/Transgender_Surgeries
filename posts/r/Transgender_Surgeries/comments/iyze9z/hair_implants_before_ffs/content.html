My ffs surgeon ( deschamps-braly ) strongly urges to not get hair grafts prior to ffs as there is risk to forehead vascularization which can impact ffs procedures involving the forehead down the line.

I do not have any reviewed papers unfortunately validating this - he does expound on his viewpoint on his site however https://deschamps-braly.com/facial-feminization-surgery/hair-transplants/ wherein all the usual caveats regarding non peer reviewed opinion pieces reside.
