Funky smells can go either way. The easiest way I found to deal with them is a very weak betadine (iodine) solution in the douche. I think I used about 10 drops of 1% iodine (10% povidone-iodine) in 1L - when you mix it, the water is still virtually clear. You don't want an infection. I've had an infection a few days after noticing very funky smell. It wasn't pretty. After fever started, it spread to my intestine in a couple days resulting in a week's worth of apocalyptic diarrhea on top of everything else. You don't want that, but the preventative is pretty simple.
