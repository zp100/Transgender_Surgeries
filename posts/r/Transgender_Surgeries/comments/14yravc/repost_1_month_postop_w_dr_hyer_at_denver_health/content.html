It's been a whole month! Well, technically 34 days so I'm a little late on posting this.

Currently recovery is going great, after the 2nd week things really began to feel better in terms of physical recovery and what I'm able to do again. At this point I can do pretty much anything outside of strenuous movement/exercise. Sleep is finally undisturbed as I can sleep in whatever position and feel comfortable. I'm very eager to see what everything's going to like around the 3 month mark! I seem to be healing fairly well, my outside sutures are taking the slowest it feels like because they just look angry still, but I'm doing what I can to promote healing. The discharge around my clitoris is also ~bleh~ but I know in time that'll clear up too. Discharge from my vaginal canal was pretty rough from Week 3-4, but I started using significantly less lube for dilation and it helped clear that up a lot. Things feel good!

I have my 6-week post-op visit next week and I'm looking forward to that. It'll be nice to get an update on things. I probably won't be making another post until my 3 month, but will be taking progress pics along the way! If you have any questions, feel free to ask or DM me! <33
