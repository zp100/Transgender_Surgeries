Watch it closely. I had a hematoma too, but it ruptured about day 5 and opened the suture line back up a little (it was messy). Then it got infected. After six months, it was still open.

They had to go back in, debride the dead tissue, pack the cavity with Cutimed Sorbact ribbon, and start me on wound care treatment. I had to go in three times/week for about six weeks to get the wound repacked. I really regret going six months untreated.
