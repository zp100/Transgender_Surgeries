According to Dr. Will Powers (and I agree with him 100% on this):

For PI: Dr. Richard Santucci of Crane Center

For PPT: Dr. Rachel Bluebond-Langner and Dr. Lee Zhao of NYU Langone

For Colon and Vulva repairs: Dr. Geoffrey Stiller

My personal experience, I’ve known two girls personally who have been butchered by Rumer and have gotten revisions from RBL/Zhao at NYU Langone and good god the team worked their magic and fixed Rumers mess! Note that NYU Langone has an extremely long wait along with other well known PPT surgeons like Wittenberg, Jun, Dy, etc.

A good back up would be Dr. Stranix and Corbett at UVA, just recently seen some work from them and they are very good about surgical revisions despite them being more new compared to the other surgeons in the list. (Edited: Dr. Stranix was trained by Bluebond!)
