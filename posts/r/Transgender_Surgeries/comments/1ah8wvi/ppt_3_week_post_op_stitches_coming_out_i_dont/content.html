Try to relax. The stitches are supposed to come out. If they pop too early, that is usually a very common complication that will heal on its own eventually.

It’s fair that you’re anxious about a revision, but the vast vast majority of complications won’t require one. Try not to worry about that possibility until your doctor says you need one—very unlikely.
