Ignore the flecks of baby wipe >_>

All things considered, I am extremely lucky to have the result I do. Firstly, I got to keep my pubes because my surgery was on such short notice, since Dr Fahradyan is Mayo Clinic's new hire. He just cauterized the follicles in the most important areas, and for the most part the hair hasn't come back. Second, I lost a considerable amount of tissue to necrosis in the first month, and for a while there this whole region was bare flesh. After a couple months of scar cream, my scars are only visible from certain angles. Such a relief. Can't wait to see how I'll look after 1 year. I'm so so happy with my new body. It was a bumpy ride but 100% worth it.
