I was there last February for my surgery and it went well. First, make sure you connect with the front desk as they will provide you with all the logistical steps. They are extremely well informed and may even give you some paperwork and the time that the taxi will pick you up to bring you to the clinic on your operation day. The front desk will also give you some Uber eats credit so you can eat while you’re staying at the hotel. I believe they give you a $50 credit per day.

For any of your pre-surgery procedures you need to read booklet C which has all the instructions on your enema, bacterial wash and putting Nair or Veet on your genitals to remove the hair. Make sure you follow these instructions so it’s not to delay or postpone your surgery. Particularly with eating you do not want to have too much food around dinner time the night before your surgery so it’s you’re not bloated from the painkillers after your surgery, and also make sure you do not eat and drink at 8 to 12 hours prior to your surgery.

Then make sure when you get up on the day of your surgery you were on time, you bring all your luggage with you out of the hotel and be ready for the start of your new journey.
