He is very aggressive and gets good results for many people. I personally don't feel comfortable because I've read an inoordinate amount of reviews where people have complications, including infections, lumps, unnecessary scarring, etc.

Look on all reviews for him under realself, sort by lowest review. Do the same thing on yelp. I mean just do some googling on him and also google his practice names. He practices under both his name and a name for his office (Back Bay Plastic surgery for the Boston office and Uptown Plastic Surgery is his NYC office.

I'm not worried about results, he gets good results. I'm worried about complications, safety, protocol, pre-surgery care and aftercare. I am still tempted to go to him, but given some of what I'm read I don't know if my comfort level is going to let me pull the trigger.
