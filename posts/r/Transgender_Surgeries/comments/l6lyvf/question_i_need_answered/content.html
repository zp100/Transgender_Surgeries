Yes, it definitely happens. You want to do electrolysis on any of the areas the surgeon tells you to. The surgeon typically tries to kill any stray hairs they find during the procedure. It is a good idea to finish your electrolysis a couple months prior to surgery. This will give time for the hairs that are not dead to reappear. Otherwise the surgeon might not be able to find them.
