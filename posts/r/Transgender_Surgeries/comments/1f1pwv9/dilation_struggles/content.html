It is going to sound counter-intuitive, but try squeezing your pelvic muscles (kegals) while you have the dilator in. Hold them for a count of 5, then relax while breathing out and giving the dilator a bit of pressure. Do this 2-3 times in a row and see if it helps. This will be a bit uncomfortable, but it should not cause any significant pain.
