Basically what the title says. I created an alt account for this post, but I really wanted to share my experience with the program at Temple, since it's still on the new side and there wasn't much out there about it when I was looking.

So, I'm currently 2 weeks post op and 1 week post discharge. I feel like first and foremost, I have to say that Dr. Douglass and Dr. Hamidian were absolutely fabulous. Both of them went out of their way to make me feel comfortable and cared for, not to mention I'm beyond happy with my results.

The gender affirming dept at Temple has also been really great. I appreciated how many queer folks were actually working in the department, which made it feel less sterile and detached. Of course, their still a new program and Temple certainly isn't the best hospital system around, but I never experienced poor care, it just felt less polished overall.

But, honestly at this point in time I would choose this team again in a heartbeat.

Not sure if I got everything, but feel free to ask questions and I'll do my best to answer them!
