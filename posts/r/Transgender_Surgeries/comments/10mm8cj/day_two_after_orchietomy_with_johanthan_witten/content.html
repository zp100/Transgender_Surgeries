Surgery went well. I had trouble sitting up. They removed a fist sized hydrocele and a conected string of smaller cysts with the orchietomy.

Scheduling was rough, i had to call and email several times to get information plus some of my own blunders that made it difficult since i wasnt taking notes. Their office is very busy so you have to stay on top of them for paperwork, but dont let disscourage you.

Johnathan is very personable but brief, write down questions before the consulation. The surgery team was excellent. The operation was only about an hour then i was sent home.

The pain has been tolerable. Most of it in my bladder.. it was pretty full after surgery, the rest in my hips and stomach kind of similar to having cramps.

The second day has been more of the same but ive been able to walk to the bathroom without assistance a little easier. Havent showered yet. No swelling yet either

Ive really only took the oxycodone to sleep at night. It really hasnt been all that vital.
