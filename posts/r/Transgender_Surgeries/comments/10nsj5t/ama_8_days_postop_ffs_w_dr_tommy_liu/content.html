I had FFS Stage 1 (Type 3 forehead, brow lift and shave, and chin and jaw contouring) with Dr. Tommy Liu at the Polyclinic in Seattle 8 days ago!

My swelling peaked on Day 2 and I've barely had any bruising at all. My chin and jaw are still pretty swollen, but my forehead is coming along really well (and it looks freaking perfect btw). I have full sensation on the skin of my forehead, mouth, chin, and jaw, but I'm still dealing with some subdermal numbness and muscle weakness, particularly on the inside of my mouth. That said, I've been feeling my nerves healing every day (pins and needles, stingers, all that jazz), so it probably won't be long until I'm back to normal on that front.

Dr. Liu told me I'm healing well ahead of schedule, so that's awesome! Gonna get Part 2 (septorhinoplasty, cheek fat transfer, and tracheal shave) scheduled here in the next week!

Dr. Liu is honestly the best. I'm so glad I went to him. And you should too! He's pretty undiscovered as far as FFS surgeons go, so you can get in pretty quickly :)
