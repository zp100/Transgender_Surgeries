I started feeling very bloated, and realized way too late than the catheter bag was not filling up at all. I was too slow and ended up going to get urgent care to the clinic, as you might imagine today it was closed and it was quite a hassle to get there and a nurse.

Holy shit it did hurt, about 2 hours of pure agony. If i had known what was happening all of this would have been much easyer

Im okay now, in fact i feel great, but i have to warn you.

If you feel bloated, and the catheter seems to not be working. Thereis a part of the tube you can deatach, do it and hopefully you'll drain out. If it doesen't, go to the clinic. They'll try to push the catheter a little bit more, and if that doesen't work, they'll deatach the tube again and fill you with some saline solution to get any bubbles out, and then you'll became a water hose for like 2 minutes. After that, you'll feel a lot better. And they may change the catheter.

At first i thought i had an intestinal blockage and i tryed some other stuff and thats why i took so long. Don't be like me, if you see this happening to you. Act fast and most possibly you'll be done in no time...
