I just got my bill from Mills Peninsula Medical Center for my Penile Inversion Gender Affirming Vaginoplasty surgery including a three night hospital stay.

  1. Anesthesia $7,713.00
  2. Laboratory $419.00
  3. Medical/Surgical Supplies and Devices $6,516.61
  4. Operating Room Services $32,953.00
  5. Pharmacy $6,716.60
  6. Recovery Room $2,002.00
  7. Room and Board $14,004.00

Total: $70,324.21

I'm incredibly privileged to have a job with good health insurance so I only paid a tiny fraction of that out of pocket.

Just wanted to post to help those considering their options. Here's my post about my experience in the hospital https://www.reddit.com/r/Transgender_Surgeries/comments/1faa9t7/comment/lo0az5p/
