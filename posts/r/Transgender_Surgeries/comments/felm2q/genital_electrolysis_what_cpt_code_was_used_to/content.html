Update: 03/11/20

Turns out the CPT 17380 is in fact a valid code for electrolysis, specific for genital surgery. I was called by my insurance this afternoon and informed the original agent contacted about the prior authorization for the service, did not look far enough into the coverage as it pertains to GRS. Apparently this person only looked as far as not covered for cosmetics and went no further.

Lesson learned . . . If turned down/denied using the CPT 17380, make sure they look beyond cosmetic and check the coverage as a prerequisite for GRS.

Edit 3(14/20): What turned out to be helpful when making the calls was to point the call center person to the exact pages and paragraphs in the policy brochure where the information for genital electrolysis is listed (my 2020 policy is 173 pages). It saved them time not having to search and allowed them to quickly cross-reference to support me. It had the added advantage of letting them know they were working with someone who had done my homework.
