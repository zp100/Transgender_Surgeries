Please don't use any type of hair removal chemical inside your vulva, you'd likely burn yourself even if fully healed (not to mention complications while still healing and the damage you could do to your urinary tract). Presuming they're just inside the vulvar area, electrolysis will be your best bet as it's perfect for having a few stray hairs removed (since electro destroys the follicle in a targeted manner). I'd still consult your surgeon even if so though on how long you'd want to be healed before doing electrolysis (I'd bet they'll suggest closer to the 6 month mark at least). Unfortunately the only thing I can think of in the meantime is to try to clip the hairs as short as you can.
