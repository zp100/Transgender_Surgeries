It's mostly the surgery, honestly. I did absolutely zero voice training before surgery. Post-surgery I have met with a speech therapist who specializes in trans women who have had voice feminization surgery. I've met with her three times so far and they've definitely been helpful but Even prior to seeing her my results were pretty similar. She's helped me with being able to not slip back down into lower ranges and also being able to access even higher ranges by getting loud and working on breath support and placement of the larynx and formation of vowel sounds and how I shape my mouth and those sorts of things.
