I came to Thailand alone and when I arrived at the airportat around 6pm. kamol team sent someone to pick up you at the airport. Basically there is nothing you need to worry about. They will just drive you to K Garden directly.

After seeing dr kamol, i was send to do x ray and blood test. The nurse is quick and professional. After these were done, I was free to go.

At the end I just chose to keep the room as i knew I may not be so capable of packing and unpacking after the surgery.

I only brought underwear, laptop, iphone and charger and some clothes just as spare. Then they directly sent me to the hospital room. They would change the dressing twice a day (no pain at all). And there r 3 meals a day, i can chose mostly soup and no normal food was allowed.

The pain was acceptable and the level of pain slowly reduced. The first 2 days I got mophine every 3~4 hours and after that normally painkiller is ok for me.

I am able to stand and walk for few steps on day 3 and able to poo on day 4 (no pain). I was discharged from the hospitsl on day 5 and back to my hotel room.

On day 7 my catheter was removed. However , before removal of the catheter i was required to drink a lot of water and they only removed my catheter after making sure i can pee. The first time i pee as a woman was amazing (no pain). The removal of catheter did cause little pains but i was fine after few hours.

After the removal of catheter, i was pretty much back to normal, except for i cant sit for a long time. I can walk quite normally . This is improving day by day and on day 8 day would remove thr stitches . At around day 10, i felt 80% recovered and pain free already.
