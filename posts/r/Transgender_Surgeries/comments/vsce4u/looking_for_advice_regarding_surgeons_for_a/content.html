Hey there, saw this post just now so not sure if you are still looking for advice. But for posterity, i decided to comment anyway :)

I did not have srs with Dr Gurjala but I did have a secondary BA with him and I am super happy with the results. His stitch work is amazing!

That aside, purely based on my 2 consults with him, a quick meet before the surgery and the post-op followup appointments, he seemed a very calm knowledgeable empathetic person. He listened to all my concerns and answered my questions so that i fully understand the surgery. The day that i was gonna have the procedure, I actually started panicking while discussing implant sizes for my BA. This happened because the sizes he suggested looked way too big (in my eyes) and i kept on telling him that i wanted a natural look. He did a good job at calming me down, explained why he was suggesting that size and assured I would be happy. Fast forward to present day, I couldn't be any happier and I'm glad Dr Gurjala used the size he used on me.

Hope this helps!
