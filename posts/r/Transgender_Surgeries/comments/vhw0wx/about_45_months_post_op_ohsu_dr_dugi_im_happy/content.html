I think he and the entire team are great. I had an absolutely lovely experience.

Sensation has been incredible. I feel I should provide some context though as well, climax has never been easy for me to achieve. It got a lot harder about a year into my transition. Before srs I could likely count on one hand for the past two years.

What I will say is that self exploration feels much more right. I don't often have a defined end point or a climax per say, but I can really build up some amazing feelings. For me it's pretty much night and day. I'm just lost in sensation and feeling it everywhere in my body. I'm not distracted in the way I used to be.

I orgasmed for the first time about 10 weeks after. I'm still figuring out what works for me, but it's night and day for me. I'm always enjoying myself and even if climax doesn't happen it doesn't feel so far away. My clit was a little too sensitive initially, but it's getting much much nicer. I have zero regrets about going with Dugi.

His staff does a great job laying out expectations which reeeeeally helped me have a good mindset through recovery. One thing I'd definitely try to keep in mind is that fear and pain in recovery are powerful and will make you believe the worst. Don't trust your thoughts in those moments. You will be fine and if things are too painful call them. Take your pain meds. Use the opiates if you need them. I was scared of them because of some family history. However, these drugs have an intended purpose, your pain should be managed so that your emotional energy can be put towards recovering.

Also very happy to answer more questions and or ramble at you if you'd just like to hear more.
