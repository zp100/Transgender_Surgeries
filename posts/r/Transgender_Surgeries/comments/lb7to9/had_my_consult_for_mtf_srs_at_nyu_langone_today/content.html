So my appointment was this morning, I arrived in NYC on Sunday and they require COVID tests before all in person visits, so I got that done on Monday in prep for my appointment!

I arrived at NYU Langone at about 9 a.m. this morning (my appointment was at 9:30) and they were running a little bit behind this morning (I’m guessing because the whole city was basically shut down yesterday due to the snowstorm) so I didn’t get called back until about 10:15.

So I first met with a nurse who took my blood pressure and went over some basic info, medical history, etc. normal intake stuff. She left and after a few minutes, Dr. Shakir came in.

Dr. Shakir is a urologist who from what I gather works under Dr. Zhao. He came in and went over some more intake stuff, asked me about my current health, sexual function, and offered to answer any questions I had in order to insure that Dr. Bluebond-Langner and Dr. Zhao weren’t in there all day with me haha. Dr. Shakir was able to answer basically all of my questions, and he gave me a gown to change into for the physical examination, and excused himself.

Not too long after that, Dr. Bb-L and Dr. Zhao entered and spoke to me about everythinggggg surrounding surgery, looked at my surgery site, and said that I’m basically ready for surgery and to just continue with electrolysis for the smaller, light hairs (I sent all of my letters and forms through the MyChart and brought the ones I couldn’t fill out virtually to the appointment). So yay! They then showed me TONS of pictures (all of which where incredible, btw. It’s hard to find results sometimes that aren’t like 0-3 months post op, so it was nice to see so many different results in different stages of recovery) and we talked about aesthetics, and they said I would receive a call in the next week or two with a date for surgery!! They are booking out about a year, but with cancellations I may be able to get a closer date!

All in all, the consultation was VERY in depth, I had no questions after, and they made me feel so welcome and safe. I’m already thrilled with the care they’ve shown me, and I haven’t even had surgery yet.

If anyone has any questions, I would be more than happy to answer if I can! AMA!
