As a massage therapist, this sounds like scarring or swollen lymph nodes (pronably both). Scar tissue can exist under the skin, and even if it's not visible it can block up your bloodflow, causing inflammation, waste buildup, and slowed healing. Lymphatic drainage massage can help clear out waste and reduce lymph nodes. It can be expensive, but maybe you can find sliding scale options in your queer community. Regular massage can break up tension and scarring, and improve circulation to assist healing. You should ideally get cleared by a doctor, but I'm sure it depends on the therapist whether they'd be willing to work on you at this stage.
