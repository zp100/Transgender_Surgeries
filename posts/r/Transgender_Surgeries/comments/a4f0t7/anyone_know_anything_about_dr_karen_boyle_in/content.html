Update: I spoke with Dr. Boyle’s office and while they perform vaginoplasties, they only perform them on cisgender women (vaginal rejuvenation), and not transgender women (GCS). She is a urologist, and has operated with Dr. Bluebond-Langner on MtF vaginoplasties, but only performing the orchiectomy (which her staff graciously offered as a service to me). She did this when Dr. Bluebond-Langner was based in Maryland, but hasn’t participated in a MtF vaginoplasty since.

So, I’m back talking with my insurance asking them to explain this referral and to move my case along. Funny quote of the day was when one of the customer care people at my insurance told me (in response to the information that Dr. Boyle doesn’t perform MtF vaginoplasty) “that can’t be; the authorizations department thoroughly researches these things before they give their responses!” Umm, well.... ¯_(ツ)_/¯
